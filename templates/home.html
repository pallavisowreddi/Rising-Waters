Technical Description

Advanced Weather Pattern Analysis

Floods are among the most destructive natural disasters, often leaving emergency coordinators with inadequate response windows. Conventional models fail to capture localized seasonal variance.

Our system implements highly optimized supervised machine learning classifiers to predict flood probabilities from 5 main atmospheric indicators: **Cloud Cover, Annual Rainfall, Jan-Feb, Mar-May, and Jun-Sep rainfall distribution**.

Disaster Scenarios

How the System is Utilized

Our scalable early warning framework serves meteorologists, emergency dispatch teams, and government data analysts.

1. Early Warnings

Meteorologists log current metrics for high-risk regions to evaluate predictions and issue critical evacuation advisories hours in advance.

2. Resource Standby

Disaster relief coordinators monitor multiple districts simultaneously during the monsoon season to prioritize resource allocation.

3. Model Validation

Government analysts run historical inputs against the models to compare accuracy, verifying the application's operational readiness.
